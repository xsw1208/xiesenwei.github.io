M4U is a benchmark for multilingual, multi-discipline multimodal understanding
and reasoning. It evaluates whether large multimodal models can jointly handle
visual evidence, language understanding, and subject-specific reasoning across
multiple languages.

- Venue: WACV 2026
- Project page: [M4U Benchmark](https://m4u-benchmark.github.io/m4u.github.io/)
- Code: [GitHub](https://github.com/M4U-Benchmark/M4U)
- Paper: [CVF Open Access](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/WACV2026/html/Wang_M4U_Evaluating_Multilingual_Understanding_and_Reasoning_for_Large_Multimodal_Models_WACV_2026_paper.html)
