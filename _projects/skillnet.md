SkillNet studies how reusable skills can help vision-language-action models
generalize to unseen task compositions and behavior categories. It models skill
attributes hierarchically and uses skill-contextualized mixture-of-experts
routing to encourage transferable expert activations across related skills.

- Venue: ICML 2026
