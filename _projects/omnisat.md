OmniSAT studies compact action tokenization for auto-regressive
vision-language-action models. It introduces an Omni Swift Action Tokenizer to
compress continuous robot actions into transferable discrete tokens, reducing
sequence length while preserving reconstruction quality and improving training
efficiency.

- arXiv: [2510.09667](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.09667)
