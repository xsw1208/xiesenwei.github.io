ARSlice is a head-mounted display system augmented with dynamic tracking and
projection. It combines optical design, high-speed sensing, and projection
control to support interactive augmented-reality display experiences.

- Journal: Journal of Computer Science and Technology, 2022
- DOI: [10.1007/s11390-022-2173-y](https://doi.org/10.1007/s11390-022-2173-y)
